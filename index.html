About

Short — 1 sentence
KcalzCounter is an AI-powered calorie and macro tracker for iOS that turns a photo of your meal into a full nutrition log in seconds.
Medium — 2 sentences
KcalzCounter is a privacy-first calorie tracker for iOS, built around an AI food-scan camera that identifies your meal and logs calories, protein, carbs and fat in seconds. It syncs with Apple Health, tracks water and weight, and keeps every byte of your personal data on-device.
Long — press boilerplate
KcalzCounter is an AI-powered nutrition tracker for iPhone that removes the single biggest reason people quit calorie counting: manual entry. Users point the camera at their plate, and the app returns an identified meal, portion estimate and full macro breakdown in seconds. Built in Swift as a single-developer indie app, KcalzCounter integrates with Apple Health, supports Home Screen widgets, and ships in English and Brazilian Portuguese. Privacy is a core design constraint: personal profile, meals, weight history and HealthKit data stay on the device — KcalzCounter does not operate a backend that stores user health data.
